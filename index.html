ABOUT US

Welcome to our fence company based in Bear, Delaware! We are a locally owned and operated business that has been serving the community for several years. Our team is passionate about providing top-quality fencing solutions for both residential and commercial properties.

At our company, we believe that fences not only provide security and privacy but also add aesthetic value to your property. That's why we offer a range of fencing options, including vinyl, wood, aluminum, and chain link, to cater to your unique needs and preferences.
